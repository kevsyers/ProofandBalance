Our Story

Proof & Balance was founded on a simple belief that financial clarity should never feel out of reach.

Built on Trust, Clarity, and Partnership

Running a business already comes with enough complexity. Your bookkeeping should provide stability, structure, and visibility—not confusion. At Proof & Balance, we establish and maintain organized financial processes that help business owners understand their numbers and move forward with confidence.

Our firm was built on a shared vision between two experienced accounting professionals who believe the best financial partnerships are built on reliability, transparency, and genuine support.

Jennifer Syers

Co-Owner, Proof & Balance

Hi, I’m Jennifer — one of the owners of Proof & Balance.

I’m a wife to my husband Kevin (he’s a nerd in the best possible way) and a mom to two boys, Gideon and Caleb. Our family has called Greenville and Winterville home for the past 20 years, and we truly love being part of this community.

I graduated from East Carolina University with a degree in accounting and bring more than 20 years of bookkeeping experience working with businesses across a wide range of industries. Over the years, I’ve become especially comfortable stepping into disorganized books, cleaning them up, and establishing processes that keep everything consistent moving forward.

Melissa Trevino

Co-Owner, Proof & Balance

Hi, I’m Melissa — one of the owners of Proof & Balance.

My husband Irl and I are happily blended with our three adult sons — Guillermo III, Evan, and Julian. We’ve recently entered the grandparent phase with our first grandson, Guillermo IV, who already shares his dad’s big personality and charm. Watching that connection grow has been one of the sweetest joys of this new chapter of life.

I earned my accounting degree from the University of Texas at San Antonio and have spent nearly 25 years building a career focused on financial accuracy, organization, and client peace of mind.

My career began in both commercial and residential property management and real estate accounting before expanding into private equity and trust accounting, where I spent the majority of my career. Over time, I also worked extensively with joint venture and franchise accounting, which provided a strong understanding of the financial needs of growing businesses.

What I enjoy most is being a trusted resource for many business owners — helping maintain organized, transparent financial records so they can stay focused on running their businesses.

That passion, along with a shared vision with a close friend and colleague, ultimately led to the creation of Proof & Balance — a firm built on personalized service, transparency, and dependable financial processes.

Outside of work, my husband and I enjoy traveling as empty nesters and experiencing new cultures, languages, and foods whenever we can. As a first-generation American with Filipino heritage, I have a special appreciation for Asian culture while also enjoying adventures around the world — including recent trips to Ireland, Italy, and Peru.
